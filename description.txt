*English Description*

I'm quite poor in English, in which case I'm so sorry to leave some grammar mistakes here.
You're welcome to try this [Turn Spears into Javelins] mod!
The mod adds "Alt Fuction Use" to all vanilla spears (or pikes or other spear-like melee weapons).
WILL make assistance to other mods (if that comes, Calamity will be the first I guess) but not until when I'm no longer lazy :)

It includes:
Spear, Trident, Swordfish, TheRottedFork
DarkLance, ObsidianSwordfish
Cobalt/Palladium/Mythril/Orchalcum/Adamantite/Titanium spear
 Gungnir, MushroomSpear
ChloropytePartisan, NorthPore

Not includes:
GhastlyGlaive, on the one hand not many people can obtain it in common playthrough, on the other hand it actually dosen't attack like normal spears
-but the most important thing is that I spent minutes looking for its inner name ending finding "MonkStaffT2". On that second I decided this weapon just should be left here without any attentions =)
ScourgeoftheCorruptor/Daybreak, they ARE javelins currently (Not going to turn javelins into spears!)
Non-melee weapons like Javelin/BoneJavelin

There WILL be balancing issues 'cause I balanced them to "Can get next spear with this one"
For examples, Swordfish and TheRottenFork can slain Skeleton, ChloropytePartisan can overwelm FrostMoon, while NorthPole butchers MoonLord easily

*中文描述*

欢迎使用【把长矛转化为投矛】模组！
此模组给所有原版长矛加入了右键使用模式
有计划支持其他模组，但是我懒了—v—

包括：
长矛，三叉戟，剑鱼，腐叉
暗黑长戟，黑曜石剑鱼
钴蓝、钯金矛，秘银、山铜矛，精金、钛金矛
冈格尼尔（神圣矛），蘑菇矛
叶绿镋，北极

不包括：
恐怖关刀，一是正常流程没人去拿，二是它攻击方式实际上并不是传统的长矛
——但最重要的是我花了十几分钟找它的内部名字最后发现是“MonkStaffT2（二阶和尚法杖）”。在那一刻我觉得这武器就该丢在没人想得起来的地方吃灰
腐化灾兵和破晓，它们本身就是投矛（我并不打算给投矛加入长矛的使用方法！）
标枪，骨质标枪等等连战士武器都不是的东西

【会有】平衡问题，因为我是按能拿到下一把矛的强度计算的
比如剑鱼和腐叉能杀死骷髅王，叶绿镋能打霜月，而北极能够击杀月球领主（大概）
